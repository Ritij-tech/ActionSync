Overview
ActionSync, based in India and developed by Neocortex AI Pvt Ltd, is a unified workplace AI assistant designed to enhance productivity by integrating seamlessly with various SaaS applications. The company aims to empower businesses of all sizes by providing innovative tools that simplify data access and improve operational efficiency.

Products & Services
ActionSync offers a range of services focused on enterprise search and data management. Their platform allows users to connect multiple applications, enabling a unified search interface that retrieves information across various data sources. Key offerings include:

Personal Search: A free plan for individual users to integrate apps and documents.
Team Search: An enterprise plan for teams, priced at $8 per user per month, which includes on-prem deployment support.
Personalized + Team Search: A more advanced plan at $12 per user per month, offering customized search results and enhanced data control.
